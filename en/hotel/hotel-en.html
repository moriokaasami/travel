HOTELS

Kawasaki King Skyfront Tokyu Rei Hotel

If you choose a room on the airport side of the hotel, you can see the Haneda runway. There is a large public bath and a lobby with a ping-pong table, hammocks, and picture books for children to relax and unwind. The hotel also offers a full range of amenities and a bus service for early morning access to Haneda Airport, making it a great place to stay before an early morning flight out of Haneda Airport. This time, I took advantage of a plan in cooperation with the airport's Excel Hotel's Flyer's Table, and after arriving by shuttle bus, I was able to have a leisurely breakfast at the Excel Hotel Tokyu near the ANA boarding gate.
[Official Website]　https://www.tokyuhotels.co.jp/kawasaki-r/

　　

Midorinokaze Resort Kitayuzawa

Midorinokaze Resort Kitayuzawa has a large open-air bath, a large bathhouse, and "baths of your choice" with seasonal and changing baths, you will be able to enjoy a unique bathing experience to the fullest. Snow baths in winter are also fun. You will be surprised to find your hair frozen to a crisp when you step outside. The guest rooms are relatively spacious and ideal for family trips. There is also a shuttle bus service that connects Shin-Chitose and Sapporo by reservation for convenient access. I was also surprised at the amount of snow on the snowy road on the way to and from the hotel. The buffet-style entertainment and live restaurant offers seasonal vegetables, seafood, and roast beef depending on the season. The friendly breakfast buffet staff told us that the hot springs in Kita-Yuzawa have won awards.
[Official Website]　https://midorinokaze-resort.com/

Premier Hotel Nakajima Park Sapporo

Located within walking distance to Susukino and a 4-minute walk from Nakajima Koen Subway Station, this 25-story high-rise city hotel is adjacent to the natural beauty of Nakajima Park. Guests on the upper floors can enjoy the view from the windows. Nakajima Park offers seasonal attractions, and in January, walking skis are available for rent. Although the use of playground equipment is prohibited during the snow season, parents and children can enjoy a real snowball fight. The hotel is a bit far from Sapporo Station, but if you walk a bit, you can enjoy gourmet food at restaurants in Susukino. The lounge is open to guests, and depending on the time of day, hot soup is served and wine is available as a free drink, so we were able to enjoy a moment before dinner.
[Official Website]　https://premier.premierhotel-group.com/nakajimaparksapporo/

Premier Hotel-TSUBAKI-Sapporo

Located within walking distance to Susukino, yet facing the Toyohira River, this urban resort is blessed with greenery and water. All rooms are over 36 square meters and are spacious. The breakfast restaurant is the Michelin-awarded restaurant, and like Premier Hotel Nakajima Koen, you can enjoy a glass of wine in the morning. I could not finish it in one day, so I think it would be good to enjoy it in consecutive nights. The premium bread, Tsubaki Bread, is also served at the sister restaurant, Premier Hotel Nakajima Koen. My son loved the premium bread and looked forward to breakfast both days.
[Official Website]　https://tsubaki.premierhotel-group.com/sapporo/

Shateraise Gateaux Kingdom Sapporo

This hotel is managed by Chateraise, a famous confectionery company. A free shuttle bus service is available from Sapporo Station, and day trips to the pool and hot springs are also possible. The rooms are relatively spacious, making it a popular resort for families with children. The atrium can be seen from the transparent elevator. The buffet hall offers soft-serve ice cream making for children and a wide variety of desserts. Recommended for family trips with children up to elementary school age.
[Official Website]　http://www.gateauxkingdom.com/
